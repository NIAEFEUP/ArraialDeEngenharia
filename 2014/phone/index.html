About festival

Indiefest is an alternative music festival that brings the newest, most interesting and mind-blowing artists from Europe to Tartu. Indiefest will liberate you from your everyday surroundings – we will welcome artists to Tartu, whom you might never even heard of, but have always wanted to discover.

We reveal the hidden sides of the independent culture, the exiting ideas that otherwise could just be left in to someone´s drawer or garage. The artists who you will see are true to the music in their hearts, mainstream is not something they are drawn to. We will take a ride on the wild side and we are inviting all of you to join us to enjoy the freedom – the independence.
